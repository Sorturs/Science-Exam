CHEMISTRY
Density Formula: D = m / V Atom Parts: Protons (+) & Neutrons (0) in nucleus. Electrons (-) in shells. Bohr-Rutherford: Shell 1 holds 2. Shell 2 holds 8. Physical Change: No new substance (Ice melting). Chemical Change: New substance (Rusting).
ELECTRICITY
Ohm's Law: V = I x R I (Current): Amps (Flow)
V (Voltage): Volts (Pressure)
R (Resistance): Ohms (Friction)
Series: One loop. Parallel: Many loops.
ECOLOGY
Bioaccumulation: Toxins build up in one animal. Biomagnification: Toxins get higher as you go up the food web. PMAT (Mitosis): Prophase, Metaphase (Middle), Anaphase (Away), Telophase (Two).
ASTRONOMY
Solar Eclipse: Moon is in the middle (blocks Sun). Lunar Eclipse: Earth is in the middle (shadow on Moon). Planets: Inner = Terrestrial/Rocky. Outer = Gas Giants.
